The Wallet I Found

One afternoon after school, I walked home through a small park near my house. The weather was calm and the park was not very crowded.

While walking, I noticed on the ground near a bench. When i got closer, I realized it was a brown wallet.

I picked it up and checked inside to see if there was an ID card. I wanted to know who the owner was.

Inside the wallet, I found some money and an Identity card. The Owner's address was not very far from my house.

Then, I went to the address with my father. We knocked on the door and a man opened it.

When i gave him the wallet, He looked very surprised and thankful. He said he had been looking for it since the morning.

I felt very happy because I could help someone that day, It was a simple moment, but it made me proud of doing the right thing.
