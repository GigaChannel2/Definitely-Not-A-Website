An Imagination

Headspace?

A place where you can be free from the real world, and just let your imagination run wild.
A place where you can be anything you want, and do anything you want.
A place where you can be yourself, and not care about what others think.
A place where you can be happy, and not care about the past or the future.

But where will you go when you want to leave?
Where will you go when you want to face reality again?
Where will you go when you want to be with others again?
Where will you go when you want to be alone again?
